# FP
For every intramural sport, varsity athletes of that sport are of course banned. However, many people have perhaps played that sport in high school, at the club level in college, or have played a different sport at a high level such that skills could transfer. As proud Trees, Ari and I plan to create a database of the sports Currier residents have played in high school and college, and an algorithm to optimize the IM team for a particular sport. For instance: say someone was a top track and field athlete in high school. As long as they can catch a football (which can be verified manually after the fact), they would probably be a good pick for an IM flag football team!
